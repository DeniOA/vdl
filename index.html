About VDL

The world is evolving rapidly towards mobile and digital formats for many everyday tasks, identification now being one. VDL, virtual driver's license, offers a more secure and convenient solution to confirmation of identity and right to drive a vehicle. VDL gives users the convenience of traveling lighter with a digital driver's license which is updatable on phone.

User Friendly

VDL is easy to use and convenient. Install the app on your phone, have your credentials on and display the information when you need to and share information only when you want to.

Secure

VDL is far less susceptible to fraud than a typical physical driver's license. It provides high level security in credential storage, data transmission and verification as you control when to show it and to whom.

Offline Sharing

VDL can be presented as at when needed for identification by sharing to whoever in offline mode.

Infractions - Tickets

Law enforcement bodies can sanction law offenders for offenses committed and selecte applicable offense as listed in the app for any offender.

Renewal of License

VDL allows users to easily renew expired license within the app whenver necessary.

Payments

Government bodies or law enforcement bodies can ensure law offender pay for crimes committed with the app and confirm payment instantly.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Register basic information as required in the provided fields to create an account.

Upload

Upload and fill in informations needed in cases of incomplete database information.

Launch

Launch the app and authenticate.

Display

Display the license when you need to, and share information only when you want to.
